# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Event hosts in Little Rock and surrounding areas who need mobile bartending for weddings, private parties, corporate events, and celebrations. They are comparing vendors, checking trust signals, and looking for a quick path to request pricing and availability.

## Product Purpose

The site presents In The Mix as a polished mobile bartending service and converts visitors into booking inquiries through a clear event request form.

## Brand Personality

Bold, lively, precise. The brand should feel like a well-run private bar: energetic enough for a party, organized enough for a high-stakes event.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic beige wedding-vendor styling, templated card grids without atmosphere, and low-contrast nightlife pages where the photos carry all the work but the copy becomes hard to read.

## Design Principles

Show the drink experience immediately.
Keep booking close at hand.
Balance nightlife energy with vendor credibility.
Make Little Rock feel explicit, not incidental.
Let the form feel complete without feeling bureaucratic.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, clear form labels, reduced-motion support, and language that welcomes both intimate gatherings and large events.
