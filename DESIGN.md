# Design

## Visual Theme

In The Mix uses a dark, high-contrast nightlife surface with bright lime as the primary action color and hot pink as a sparing accent. Imagery is drink-forward: splashes, citrus, bar pours, and close-up event energy.

## Color

- `--bg`: near-black, slightly green-tinted
- `--surface`: blackened charcoal
- `--ink`: clean white
- `--muted`: cool gray with enough contrast on dark surfaces
- `--lime`: electric citrus action color
- `--pink`: cocktail splash accent

## Typography

Use a narrow, condensed display voice for the logo and hero, supported by a clean sans-serif system stack for readable body and form content. Uppercase is reserved for short labels and navigation.

## Components

- Sticky dark header with logo, anchors, and booking action
- Full-bleed hero with oversized headline, service bullets, and visual cocktail crop
- Services band with icons and short service names
- Image-led about/gallery sections
- Booking form with event details, guest count, budget, date, and notes
- Footer with social and contact links

## Motion

Motion should be crisp and restrained: subtle load-in, hover lift on actions, no layout-gating transitions, and a reduced-motion override.
